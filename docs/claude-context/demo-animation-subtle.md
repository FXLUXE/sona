Daniel wants the Sona demo backdrop **much more subtle**. The bold version (Yazz salon: plum/magenta background, big spinning shears, twinkling sparkles, vivid colours) he called "absolutely horrific — design, colours, animations, everything."

**Why:** loud colours + spinning/large motion read as cheap/garish, not premium.

**How to apply:**
- Muted, restrained palette — no vivid magenta/plum drench; keep backgrounds calm and deep, low-saturation.
- No spinning, no large/fast motion, no twinkling sparkles. Motion should be barely-there (slow drift, gentle fade), the kind you only notice if you look.
- The industry motif should be a faint texture, not a loud centrepiece.
- Err on the side of too subtle. Elegance over impact.
