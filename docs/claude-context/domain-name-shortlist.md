Daniel wants a domain matching the product (Sona = AI website assistant that reads a small UK business's site, answers customers, captures leads), **under £25/yr**. Checked live on GoDaddy 2026-06-29.

**Key finding:** `.com` is TAKEN for every Sona-word (sona/getsona/asksona → secondary market £77+ broker). UK ICP ([[demo-sites-must-be-icp]]) makes `.co.uk` on-brand, not a compromise.

**Recommended pick: `asksona.co.uk` — £12.99/yr.** "Ask Sona" = exactly what it does; the widget launch button literally says "Ask us". Under half the £25 budget.

**Alternatives (all GoDaddy-checked, ongoing renewal price):** `asksona.uk` £12.99 · `trysona.co.uk` / `getsona.co.uk` / `meetsona.co.uk` £12.99 · `asksona.net` £19.99 (global) · `asksona.org` £19.99.

Note: GoDaddy's £0.01 teaser needs a 3-yr term; the real ongoing price is the second figure (£12.99/yr).

**How to apply:** Daniel registers it himself — I cannot (entering payment / creating a registrar account is prohibited). Once chosen + deployed, set `PUBLIC_BASE_URL` to the live domain (demo/outreach links are localhost until then — see [[sona-build-state]]).
