Daniel is non-technical. Doing technical work does NOT mean he follows the technical terms. Explain everything in plain English, no jargon, at all times — across ALL projects, old and new, not just this one.

**Why:** he's said he doesn't understand it all even while the technical work happens; jargon loses him.

**How to apply:**
- Default to plain language. If a technical term is unavoidable, define it in a few plain words right there.
- Describe things by what they DO for him / what he sees, not how they're built.
- When a question is complex or has trade-offs, don't ask it as one open question — break it into several multiple-choice questions (use the AskUserQuestion tool) so he can just pick.
- Keep it to outcomes and choices, not implementation detail, unless he asks to go deeper.
